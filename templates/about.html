Welcome To Fake Image Detection

we are dedicated to providing state-of-the-art solutions for detecting fake or manipulated images. With the rise of digital manipulation tools and the spread of misinformation, it has become crucial to develop reliable methods to discern between authentic and altered visual content Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations with tools to identify manipulated images, thereby fostering trust and integrity in digital media. Through a combination of advanced techniques such as error level analysis, gradient analysis, and metadata analysis, we aim to offer a comprehensive solution for detecting image tampering

Our Approach

Error Level Analysis:

By analyzing the error levels present in an image, we can identify regions that have undergone compression or modification, providing insights into potential areas of manipulation.

Gradient Analysis:

We leverage gradient analysis techniques to detect inconsistencies in gradients across different regions of an image, which can indicate the presence of digital alterations.

Metadata Analysis:

Examining the metadata associated with an image allows us to uncover clues about its origin, editing history, and authenticity, aiding in the detection of manipulated content.
